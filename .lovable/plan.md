### Recommended next step

**AI CV & Personal Statement Generator** would be the highest-impact feature to build next. It would:
- Use existing student profile data (name, nationality, qualifications, course interest)
- Generate a formatted CV and/or personal statement via AI (using Lovable AI supported models)
- Allow download as PDF
- Be accessible from the student detail page

Alternatively, **Task Management** would improve team coordination with a simple kanban board for owners/admins to assign and track work across agents.
